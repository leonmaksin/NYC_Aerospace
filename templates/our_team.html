Our Team
Leon Maskin: Founder & CEO

Leon Maksin is the original founder and CEO of NYC Aerospace. Beginning with his childhood dream of building and launching a rocket into outer space, Leon originally founded the Stuyvesant Rocketry Team in the Spring of 2018. Since then, Leon managed to grow the organization and help develop the multiple projects that make NYC Aerospace what it is today. Leon is also the President of the TARC and CubeSAT initiatives and helps run the other projects for NYC Aerospace.

Sofiane Larbi: Executive Vice President, President of High Power Rocketry and IAVs, & CTO

Ever since he was a little kid, Sofiane Larbi has been interested in aeronautics and technology. With a group of friends, he formed the high power rocketry team and later incorporated it into NYC Aeronautics. Sofiane Larbi built this website and manages the technological aspects of NYC Aerospace.

Jude Rizzo: CFO, Electronics President, Cohead of High Power Rocektry

Jude Rizzo is a long time math and physics enthusiast. He enjoys launching mid and high power rockets as well as building other aerial vehicles such as drones for photography.

Jakob Gorisek-Gazze: Co-head of Operations and RockED President

Jakob Gorisek-Gazze has had a lifelong love of engineering. He launched his first rockets in middle school, and has since relentlessly pursued ever more ambitious projects, be they higher, faster, or crazier.

Kristoff Misquitta: Co-Head of Operations

Kristoff Misquitta blends a passion for aerospace engineering with a love of molecular biology, tackling challenges like creating a novel morphing wing design for commercial aircraft or crafting an experiment to test PCR on the International Space Station.
