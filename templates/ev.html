Background

The Electronics department produces remote gadgets and vehicles, and was incorporated into NYC Aeropsace August, 2019. Electronics now produces electronics for other divisions and sponsors solo projects.

Mission

The mission of Electronics is to fund and support the building of drones, RC planes, weather balloons, and other individual products by students. A major project we are currently working on are building and launching a weather balloon to record atmospheric data and measurements and send back live feed to our division in the air. Electronics also aims to provide other NYC Aerospace departments with funtioning electronics.
