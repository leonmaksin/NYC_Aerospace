About Us

NYC Aerospace is a registered 501(c)(3) organization centered around bringing opportunities to students interested in the aerospace industry. We strive to create opportunities within the aerospace industries for all NYC students, regardless of class and location. NYC Aerospace is the only organization in New York City, and possibly in the United States, that provides easy access to resources for young aerospace enthusiasts. NYC Aerospace's origins come from the Stuyvesant Rocketry Team's ambitious mission to incorporate students from other schools. It was then that we realized the hidden talent many NYC students have. We have members from 9 schools and universities currently on board, as well as students participating on their own, and only hope to grow that number. We hope that NYC Aerospace will become a staple in the many opportunities New York City provides to its students.
