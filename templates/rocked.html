Background

RockED began as a realization that many students in New York City do not have proper opportunities to pursue careers in the aerospace industry. Without proper support, such interests can become nearly impossible to realize. Surrounded by tall buildings and indoor facilities, students are rarely, if ever, exposed to the wonders of the aerospace industry. RockED was founded to provide support to students interested in aerospace in New York. We hope to help you succeed.

Mission

Our goal at RockED is to provide outreach and learning opportunities to students in New York interested in the aerospace idustry. Aerospace is a growing industry, and aerospace engineering will be one of the future's most demanded skills. Since most students in New York City lack the opportunities to engage in aerospace education, we are here to give them the support they are lacking.

Get Involved!

If you have bright ideas, are interested in engineering, or are just curious, contact us and let us know! We are always happy to reach out and help you and your community pursue your passion, and we hope that you can help us foster the same passion in others! Just contact us and we will work on creating an event for your community.

What Do We Do?

We will be presenting and hosting workshops at high schools throughout New York. Topics covered include a brief introduction to NYC Aerospace and our goals, what aerospace engineering is, and most importantly, what you can do to pursue an interest through NYC Aerospace. During workshops we will introduce students to basics of engineering, physics, and rocketry through hands on activities and design competitions. You can participate and learn through other departments of NYC Aerospace, or, with our support, start you own branch for NYC Aerospace for your school or community!
