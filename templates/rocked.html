Background

RockED began as a realization. A realization that many students in New York City do not get the opportunity to pursue careers in STEM, even more so in regards to aerospace. Without proper support, such interests can become nearly impossible to realize. RockED was founded to provide this support structure to anyone who is interested in STEM and aerospace in New York. We help you succeed.

Mission

Our goal at RockED is to provide outreach and learning opportunities to students in New York in engineering and aerospace.

Get Involved!

If you have bright ideas, are interested in engineering, or are just curious, then contact us and let us know! We are always happy to reach out and help you and your community pursue your passion, and we hope that you can help us foster the same passion in others!

What Are We Doing?

We will be presenting and hosting workshops at high schools throughout New York. Topics covered include a brief introduction to NYC Aerospace and our goals, What is STEM, and most importantly, what can you do to pursue an interest, or even a career in STEM and aerospace? During workshops we will introduce students to the basics of engineering, physics, and rocketry through hands on activities and design competitions. You can participate and go on to learn in other departments of NYC Aerospace, or with our support, start you own aerospace organization at your school or with friends!
