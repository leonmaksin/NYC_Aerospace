Background

The inteligent vehicles department is working to utilize artificial inteligence and machine learning in the piloting and decision making of drones. There are many applications of this technology, but we hope to develop face tracking drones, autonomous medicine delivery drones, swarm technology, and modular space robots (beebos.)

Mission

The mission of the ineligent vehicles department is to develop useful applications of AI in aerial vehicles.
